Quantum Winter School

Exploring the Future of Quantum Computing, Quantum Machine Learning, and Quantum Sensing

Date: November 26-29, 2024 | Location: Khalifa University - Main Campus, UAE

About the Event

Join us at the Quantum Winter School for an immersive exploration into the world of Quantum Computing, Quantum Machine Learning, and Quantum Sensing. Over three days, renowned experts will guide you through foundational principles and cutting-edge advancements in quantum technology, preparing you for the future of computing. Whether you're a student, researcher, or industry professional, this workshop offers an exceptional opportunity to deepen your understanding and engage with leaders in the field.

How to Get Here to Khalifa University Main Campus

Khalifa University Main Campus is located in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The campus is easily accessible by car, public transportation, and taxi services. Please find below the campus address and directions for reaching us conveniently. You can enter from Gates 2, 6, and 7 as indicated by the red arrows. The event will hold place in R building, auditorium R02013.

Address: Khalifa University, Abu Dhabi, UAE

By Car: Enter the campus via the main gate and follow signage for visitor parking. Parking is available for attendees.

By Public Transport: The campus is accessible by several bus routes. Check local schedules for the most convenient options.

By Taxi: Taxis can drop you off directly at the main entrance. Just mention "Khalifa University Main Campus" to the driver.
